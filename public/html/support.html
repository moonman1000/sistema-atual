Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

You can easily place an order through our website or mobile app. Simply browse our menu, select your favorite items, customize them to your liking, and proceed to checkout.

What are the delivery options?

We offer standard delivery, express delivery for when you're extra hungry, and a contactless delivery option for your safety and convenience. You can select your preferred option at checkout.

Can I customize my pizza?

Absolutely! Our "Build Your Own Pizza" option lets you choose your crust, sauce, cheese, and a wide variety of fresh toppings to create your perfect pie.
